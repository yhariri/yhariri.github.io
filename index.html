About
I bring over 17 years of experience developing and deploying advanced systems at the intersection of AI, IoT, embedded computing, and the cloud-to-edge continuum. My focus is on enabling real-time, distributed intelligence by integrating AI with IoT, turning research into deployable solutions. I’m committed to building scalable, reusable platforms that empower researchers, startups, and industry to deliver meaningful impact across sectors such as green tech, EVs, smart health, precision agriculture, Industry 4.0, and defence.

Leadership & Impact
  • Leading cross-functional teams that integrate sensor systems, edge AI platforms, and real-time analytics
  • Forging strategic collaborations with industry, academia, and government through successful national initiatives
  • Advancing the innovation ecosystem via technical workshops (e.g., the annual Accelerating AI workshop 2020-2025), webinars, and GitHub projects
  • Designing and deploying AI infrastructure using GPUs, NPUs, FPGAs, and ultra-low-power AI inference accelerators
  • Providing hands-on expertise, training, and mentorship to manufacturers and startups to accelerate AI commercialization
  • Hosting flagship events uniting industry and academia around ML, AI, and hardware-software co-design
Research & Innovation

My research focuses on:

  • AI hardware acceleration and generative AI
  • Fault diagnostics in CMOS and soft error analysis in SRAM FPGAs
  • MEMS switch optimization for RF applications
These research areas are backed by a strong record of peer-reviewed journal and conference publications—see my Google Scholar profile for a full list.
